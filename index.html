About Me

I am currently a postdoc at Institut Denis-Poisson hosted by Kilian Raschel. My research interests are in algebraic combinatorics and statistical mechanics, specifically the dimer and double-dimer models.

I received my PhD in Mathematics in June 2020 from the University of Oregon, where I was advised by Ben Young.

I previously attended Whitman College where I received a B.A. in Mathematics and Psychology. I also studied at Budapest Semesters in Mathematics.
